How to Subscribe

Apple Calendar (macOS / iOS)
  1. Click the "Open in Calendar App" button above, or
  2. Open Calendar → File → New Calendar Subscription...
  3. Paste the subscription URL shown above
  4. Set auto-refresh to Every day and click Subscribe
Google Calendar
  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Click the + next to "Other calendars" in the left sidebar
  3. Select "From URL"
  4. Paste the https:// version of the URL above (not webcal://)
  5. Click "Add calendar"

Note: Google Calendar may take up to 24 hours to refresh subscription calendars.

Outlook / Microsoft 365
  1. Open Outlook Calendar
  2. Click "Add calendar" → "Subscribe from web"
  3. Paste the subscription URL shown above
  4. Name the calendar and click "Import"
